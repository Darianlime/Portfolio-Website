Ice Melts

2D puzzle platformer created with Unity
Transform between Ice • Water • Steam to solve physics-based challenges

▶ Play on Itch.io

Overview

Ice Melts is a 2D puzzle platformer developed as part of the Video Game Development Club. You play as a block of ice that can transform between Ice, Water, and Steam, each offering unique mechanics for solving environmental puzzles.

I contributed to gameplay programming, state transitions, and the chain-creator system used by designers to build puzzle interactions quickly.

What I Worked On

  • Implemented the state transformation system (Ice ↔ Water ↔ Smoke) .
  • Developed the Chain Creator Unity editor tool for puzzle scripting.
  • Worked with designers to build levels using the tool.
  • Helped polish movement, collisions, and environment interactions.

Transformation System

The core mechanic of Ice Melts is the ability to switch between three physical states, each with different movement, physics, and interactions:

  • Ice: Solid, heavy, can push objects and resist wind.
  • Water: Flows through cracks and chains, slips under obstacles.
  • Steam: Floats upward, bypasses chains, blown by fans.

Chain Creator System

I developed a custom Unity editor tool called the Chain Creator, which lets designers place chains quickly without manually building each segment.
